Environmental Gaming

Environmental gaming focuses on how the game world influences player experience. The design of landscapes, buildings, and lighting creates immersion and emotional connection.

In horror games like Reanimal, dark environments build tension and suspense. The setting becomes part of the storytelling.

Open-world games such as GTA 5 use realistic cities and interactive spaces to make players feel part of a living world.

Competitive games like Call of Duty use map design to influence strategy and movement during gameplay.

Overall, environmental design plays a major role in modern gaming because it shapes emotion, realism, and engagement.
