Environmental Gaming

Environmental gaming focuses on how the game world influences player experience. The design of landscapes, lighting, and structures creates immersion.

Horror games use dark environments to build tension and emotional impact.

Open-world games use realistic cities and dynamic weather systems to create believable worlds.

Competitive games design maps carefully to support strategy and teamwork.

Overall, environmental design enhances realism, emotion, and player engagement.
